ABOUT ME

My name is Daniel and I'm a software engineer with experience in building complex web applications using the latest technologies such as React.js, Redux.js, JavaScript, TypeScript (ect.) with the ability to connect them to a Ruby on Rails or MERN style backend.

In the solitude of the pandemic, I decided to learn some basic coding in between my college courses in order to stay productive. I soon found that I enjoyed learning to code more than I had enjoyed the college courses that I'd been taking. I love the combination of creativity and problem solving. Despite having just been accepted into UC Santa Barbara for history, I decided to pursue my newfound passion instead.

Back in 2021 I had attended and completed a highly competitive, selective, and immersive coding bootcamp called App Academy where I put in easily between 90-100+ hours a week learning an array cutting-edge technologies and applying them to both group and solo projects.

After graduating, I landed a job at database company called Timescale as a Web Developer where for over a year I was responsible for a wide range of tasks such as building/updating/maintaining web-pages, integrating APIs, managing Hubspot forms, optimizing site performance, A/B testing, building charts/dashboards for marketing analytics, and documenting stack architecture.

When I'm not busy coding, I love spending time with my close friends doing activities such as rock-climbing, making music, watching movies, and reading books on world history. Again, I love the challenge of programming because it allows me to be creative and expand the limits of my problem solving capabilities just like some of my other hobbies.
